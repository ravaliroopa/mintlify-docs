## How to Swap

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Swap">
    Go to **Swap** in the AI section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select source">
    Choose your **source chain** and **token** (e.g., ETH on Ethereum).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select destination">
    Choose your **destination chain** and **token** (e.g., USDC on Base).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount">
    Type the amount you want to swap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    Review the quote (estimated output, fees, route) and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Swap Types

- **Same-chain swaps**: Instant token-to-token swaps on the same chain
- **Cross-chain bridges**: Move tokens between different blockchains

Powered by **LiFi**, which aggregates the best rates across DEXs and bridges.

<Tip>
  Use cross-chain swaps to move funds between platforms. For example, swap ETH on Ethereum to USDC on Base for Limitless trading.
</Tip>
