## Viewing Your Portfolio

Each platform has its own portfolio page:

| Platform | Page |
|----------|------|
| Polymarket | AI section |
| Kalshi | `/kalshi-portfolio` |
| Limitless | `/limitless-portfolio` |
| Myriad | `/myriad-portfolio` |

## What You Can See

- **Open positions** with current value
- **Entry price** vs current price
- **Unrealized P&L** (profit/loss)
- **Trade history** per agent
- **Agent performance** (win rate, total P&L)

## Managing Positions

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Close Early" icon="xmark">
    Lock in profit or cut losses before a market resolves.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claim Winnings" icon="trophy">
    Redeem resolved positions. Auto-claimed by agents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cancel Orders" icon="ban">

</CardGroup>

## Tips

<Tip>
  Check your agent performance daily for the first week after enabling a new agent. This helps you understand its behavior before increasing position sizes.
</Tip>
