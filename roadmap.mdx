## Product Roadmap

Cobot is being built in phases, each unlocking a deeper level of autonomous trading capability.

### Phase 1 -- Aggregation & Intelligence

<Card title="Live" color="#22c55e" icon="check">
  Unify fragmented markets into a single interface.
</Card>

- Prediction market aggregation (Polymarket, Kalshi, Limitless, and more)
- Real-time odds, liquidity, and event tracking
- AI-powered insights and inefficiency detection
- One-click due diligence

**Goal:** Informational edge

---

### Phase 2 -- Execution Layer

<Card title="Live" color="#22c55e" icon="check">
  Move from insights to action.
</Card>

- Seamless trade execution across platforms
- Real-time signal to trade workflows
- Multi-chain wallet management (EVM \+ Solana)
- Cross-chain token swaps via LiFi

**Goal:** Reduce friction between insight and execution

---

### Phase 3 -- Autonomous Agents ( Q2 2026 )

<Card title="Current Focus" color="#8b5cf6" icon="bullseye">
  Let agents handle trading.
</Card>

- Prediction market agents (5-minute, 15-minute strategies)
- Sports AI agent with live game analysis
- User-defined risk parameters and strategy selection
- Continuous monitoring and execution
- Early performance tracking and analytics
- Expand to Kalshi and other terminals 

**Goal:** Replace manual trading

---

### Phase 4 -- Perps & Derivatives Agents ( Q2 2026 )

<Card title="Next" color="#f59e0b" icon="arrow-right">
  Expand into higher-liquidity markets.
</Card>

- Perpetual futures agents (Hyperliquid, and others)
- Directional and hedging strategies
- Volatility-based execution

**Goal:** Capture fast-moving alpha

---

### Phase 5 -- DeFi Strategy Agents ( Q3 2026 )

<Card title="Upcoming" color="#6b7280" icon="clock">
  Activate idle capital.
</Card>

- Yield optimization across protocols
- Liquidity provisioning strategies
- Cross-protocol arbitrage agents
- DeFI savings agent
- Memecoin trading agent 

**Goal:** Maximize capital efficiency

---

### Phase 6 -- Fully Autonomous Capital Layer ( Q4 2026 & beyond )

<Card title="End State" color="#6b7280" icon="infinity">
  Cobot becomes the interface for deploying capital.
</Card>

- Self-improving agents across markets
- Continuous learning from trade outcomes
- Minimal human intervention required

**Goal:** Autonomous hedge fund-like systems

## Future Direction

Cobot is evolving into a platform where:

- **Strategies are programmable** -- define your edge, let agents execute it
- **Agents are composable** -- research, execution, and risk agents work together
- **Capital is continuously optimized** -- across prediction markets, perps, and DeFi

This enables:

- **Multi-agent coordination** -- research agent feeds execution agent, which is monitored by risk agent
- **Strategy marketplaces** -- share, fork, and deploy proven trading strategies
- **Autonomous financial systems** -- capital that works 24/7 without manual oversight
