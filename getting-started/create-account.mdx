## Sign Up

1. Open the app at **[app.cobot.gg](https://app.cobot.gg)**
2. Sign in with your **phone number** (OTP) or **Google account**
3. Complete your profile (name, date of birth)

<Info>
  Cobot supports phone OTP (primary) and Google OAuth authentication. Farcaster login is also supported.
</Info>

## What Happens at Sign Up

When you create your account, Cobot automatically provisions:

- An **EVM Wallet** (Ethereum, Base, Polygon, BSC, Arbitrum) via Privy
- A **Solana Wallet** (SVM) via Privy
- A **Polymarket Safe Wallet** (Gnosis Safe on Polygon) for prediction market trading

No seed phrases to manage. Your private keys are encrypted and secured by Privy.

## Navigation

After signing in, you'll land on the **Home Dashboard** with:

- **Popular Agents** - Trending agent carousel
- **Predictions Feed** - AI-curated prediction market opportunities
- **News Feed** - Real-time market news
- **Whale Alerts** - Smart money tracking
