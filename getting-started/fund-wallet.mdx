## Funding by Platform

Each prediction market requires funds on a specific chain:

| Platform | What You Need | Where |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| **Polymarket** | USDC | Polygon (Safe wallet) |
| **Kalshi** | USDC | Solana wallet |
| **Limitless** | USDC | Base (EVM wallet) |
| **Token Swaps** | Native tokens (ETH, SOL, BNB) | Respective chain |

## Depositing to Polymarket

Polymarket uses a **Bridge** feature that accepts deposits from 9 chains:

- Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, BSC, Solana, Bitcoin, Tron

Your funds go to your Safe wallet on Polygon automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Bridge">
    Navigate to the Polymarket section and tap Bridge/Deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose source chain">
    Select the chain you're sending from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send funds">
    Transfer to the provided deposit address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation">
    Funds arrive in your Safe wallet within minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Withdrawing from Polymarket

To move USDC out of your Polymarket Safe:

1. Go to Safe Withdraw
2. Enter the amount
3. Funds transfer from Safe to your personal EVM wallet (gasless)

## Depositing to Other Platforms

For Kalshi, Limitless, and token swaps, simply send tokens directly to your wallet address shown in the Profile or Agent Wallet page.

<Tip>
  Use the **Swap** feature to convert between tokens and bridge across chains without leaving the app.
</Tip>
