**Best for:** Users who want to accumulate Kalshi trading volume.

Same concept as Polymarket Farming but targets Kalshi markets.

## Requirements

- **KYC verification** required (dFlow Proof of Personhood)
- Solana wallet must be funded with USDC

## How It Works

1. Scans Kalshi events for high-probability markets (94-98%)
2. Validates market eligibility with configurable thresholds
3. Executes small trades across multiple markets
4. Monitors with 10% stop-loss
5. Tracks performance and trade history

## Settings

| Setting | Default |
|---------|---------|
| Position size | $5 |
| Max open positions | 10 |
| Max daily loss | $100 |
| Min probability | 94% |
| Max probability | 98% |
| Max hours to expiry | 72 |
| Notifications | On |

## Page

Enable and configure at `/kalshi-farming-trading`
