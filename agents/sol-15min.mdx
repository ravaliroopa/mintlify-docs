Same architecture as the ETH 15-Minute agent but for **Solana** markets.

## How It Works

1. Fetches upcoming SOL 15-min markets on Polymarket
2. Uses **Allora SOL predictions** + baseline price
3. Determines trade direction based on predicted vs baseline price difference
4. Places trades automatically

## Allora Topics

| Asset | Timeframe | Topic ID |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| SOL | 5 minutes | 37 |
| SOL | 8 hours | 38 |

## Strategies

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Up/Down** (default) | Trades both up and down predictions |
| **Up Only** | Only trades when the AI predicts SOL will go up |
