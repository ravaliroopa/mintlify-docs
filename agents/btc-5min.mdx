**Best for:** Active traders who want to capitalize on short-term BTC price movements.

Trades BTC 5-minute prediction markets on Polymarket using real-time Binance price data and Allora AI predictions to determine trade direction.

## How It Works

1. **Market Discovery** (every 30 min): Finds upcoming BTC 5-min markets on Polymarket
2. **Signal Analysis**: Combines Binance real-time price feed with Allora AI predictions
3. **Strategy-Based Entry**: Different strategies have different entry criteria and timing
4. **Position Monitoring**: Re-evaluates positions at minute 2 and minute 3

## Strategies

Pick one strategy when enabling the agent:

| Strategy | Risk | Signal Required | Order Type |
|----------|------|----------------|------------|
| **Up/Down** | Medium | BN >= 7 bps + Allora agrees + Allora diff >= 0.1% | Market |
| **Confirmed Lead** | Medium | BN >= 7 bps + Allora agrees (override at >= 10 bps) | Market |
| **Limiter** | Lower | BN >= 11 bps + Allora agrees | Limit @ 60c |
| **Allora Strike** | Lower | BN >= 8 bps + Allora agrees (3 eval windows) | Limit @ 60c |
| **Max** | Medium | Limiter OR Allora Strike signal | Limit @ 60c |
| **Peak** | Higher | BN >= 10 bps (no Allora needed) | Market |
| **Sniper** | Highest | BN >= 25 bps (extreme momentum only) | Market |

### Signal Definitions

- **BN (Binance Lead)**: How much BTC has moved on Binance since the market reference time, in basis points. 1 bps = 0.01%.
- **Allora Agreement**: The Allora AI prediction agrees with the direction of the Binance move.
- **Allora Diff**: The percentage difference between Allora's predicted price and the current baseline.

<Info>
  **Which strategy should I pick?** Start with **Up/Down** for a good balance of trade frequency and win rate. Move to **Limiter** for more conservative entries with limit orders.
</Info>
