**Best for:** Active traders who want to capitalize on short-term BTC price movements.

Trades BTC 5-minute prediction markets on Polymarket using real-time Chainlink, binance price data and Allora AI predictions to determine trade direction.

## How It Works

1. **Market Discovery** (every 30 min): Finds upcoming BTC 5-min markets on Polymarket
2. **Signal Analysis**: Combines real-time price feed with Allora AI predictions
3. **Strategy-Based Entry**: Different strategies have different entry criteria and timing
4. **Position Monitoring**: Re-evaluates positions  

## Strategies

Pick one strategy when enabling the agent:

| Strategy | Risk | Signal Required | Order Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Up/Down** | Medium | AI signal strength 70% \+ Allora agrees \+ Allora diff \>= x | Market |
| **Confirmed Lead** | Medium | AI signal strength 70%\+ Allora agrees | Market |
| **Limiter** | Lower | AI signal strength 90% \+ Allora agrees | Limit @ 60c |
| **Allora Strike** | Lower | AI signal strength 80% \+ Allora agrees (3 eval windows) | Limit @ 60c |
| **Max** | Medium | Limiter OR Allora Strike signal | Limit @ 60c |

### Signal Definitions

- **Allora Agreement**: The Allora AI prediction agrees with the direction of the Binance move.

<Info>
  **Which strategy should I pick?** Start with **Up/Down** for a good balance of trade frequency and win rate. Move to **Allora strike** for more conservative entries with limit orders.
</Info>
