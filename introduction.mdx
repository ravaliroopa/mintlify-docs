## What is Cobot?

Cobot lets you trade on prediction markets like **Polymarket**, **Kalshi**, **Limitless**, and **Myriad**, either manually or with automated AI agents that trade for you 24/7.

You also get multi-chain crypto wallets, token swaps, market research tools, and an AI chat assistant.

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Type | Chain |
|----------|------|-------|
| **Polymarket** | CLOB (Order Book) | Polygon |
| **Kalshi** | Regulated Exchange | Solana (dFlow) |
| **Limitless** | AMM + CLOB | Base |
| **Myriad** | Prediction Market | Multi-chain |

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [app.cobot.gg](https://app.cobot.gg) with your phone number or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your wallet">
    Deposit USDC to your auto-created EVM or Solana wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    Trade manually or enable an AI agent to trade for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>
