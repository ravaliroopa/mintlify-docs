## How to Trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse events">
    Go to prediction tab and choose Myriad in the dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your side">
    Select a market and choose your side.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount">
    Set your trade amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm your trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>
