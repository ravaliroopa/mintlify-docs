Kalshi is a US-regulated prediction exchange that settles trades on Solana via dFlow.

## How to Trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse events">
    Go to **Kalshi** events in the AI section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a market">
    Browse events and markets. Each market has a Yes/No outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your order">
    Choose **Yes** or **No**, select market or limit order, set your amount in dollars or contracts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and confirm your trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

- **KYC verification** may be required (dFlow Proof of Personhood)
- Trades settle on **Solana** via dFlow
- Fund your **Solana wallet** with USDC

## Portfolio

View your Kalshi positions at the **Kalshi Portfolio** page:

- Track open positions and settlement status
- View P&L per position
- Close positions or wait for settlement

## Order Modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Dollars** | Specify trade amount in USD |
| **Contracts** | Specify number of contracts |

<Info>
  Kalshi markets use binary outcomes (Yes/No). Each contract pays $1 if correct, $0 if wrong.
</Info>
