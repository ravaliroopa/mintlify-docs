Polymarket is the largest prediction market. You bet on real-world event outcomes like elections, sports, crypto prices, and more.

## How to Trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse markets">
    Go to **Polymarket** in the AI section. Search for a topic or browse events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an outcome">
    Tap an event to see its markets. Choose **Yes** or **No** on an outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select order type">
    - **Market order**: Fills immediately at the current best price
    - **Limit order**: Set your price, fills when the market reaches it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set amount and confirm">
    Enter your amount in USD and confirm your trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Positions

- View open positions in your portfolio
- **Close early** to lock in profit or cut losses
- Winning positions are **automatically redeemed** when markets resolve
- Track **whale transactions** for market intelligence

## Order Types

| Type | How It Works | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Market** | Fills immediately at current price | Quick execution |
| **Limit** | Fills when price reaches your target | Better entry prices |

## Key Features

- Real-time orderbook data via WebSocket
- Whale transaction tracking
- Portfolio view with P&L
- Auto-redemption of winning positions
- Safe wallet for enhanced security
- Multi-chain bridge deposits

<Warning>
  Trading carries risk. You can lose your entire position if the market resolves against you.
</Warning>
